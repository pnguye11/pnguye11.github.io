BrickBreaker

BrickBreaker is my first app/game I've ever built. In the game, bricks are form in collumns and rows on the screen. A ball will drop down and once it hits the paddle the ball will travel across the screen, bouncing off the the sides of the wall on the screen. When a brick is hit, the ball bounces in the opposite directions and the brick is destroyed. The player has a paddle to manipulate the bouncing direction of the ball, avoiding the botton of the screen.
